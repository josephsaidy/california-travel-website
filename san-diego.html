San Diego

City Facts

  • Region: Southern California / Coastal
  • Classification: Urban
  • Median household income (San Diego): $104,321
  • Median household income (California): $96,334
  • Comparison: Above state average

Attractions

Balboa Park & Zoo, USS Midway Museum, and Old Town.

Parks

Mission Bay Park, Torrey Pines State Natural Reserve, and Sunset Cliffs.

Museums

San Diego Museum of Art, Museum of Us, and Maritime Museum.

Food

Fish tacos, Little Italy dining, and oceanfront brunch spots.

About San Diego

Famous for beaches, naval presence, and Balboa Park. Mild weather year-round makes it a tourist favorite.
